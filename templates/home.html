Our Services

What Child Protect Online System can do for you

Child Safe Search 

Child Safe Search uses advanced filters to protect kids from harmful online content. Parents receive notifications for concerning searches and can ban or unban keywords, fostering communication.

Education and Awareness 

This feature provides cybersecurity training for parents and children, enhancing online safety awareness and promoting responsible internet usage.

Discussion Forum 

The Discussion Forum facilitates parent-to-parent communication and knowledge sharing on child online safety, creating a supportive community.

Parental Monitoring 

Parental Monitoring offers tools for tracking a child's online activities, including emails, search history, and timestamps, ensuring a safe online experience and trust-building..
