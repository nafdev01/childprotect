What is Online Addiction?

Online addiction is a bit like when you love something so much that you can't stop doing it. Imagine if you really liked playing with your toys, and you played with them all the time, even when you should be eating or sleeping. It's a bit like that but with the internet. It's when someone spends too much time online, even when they don't need to.

Signs of Online Addiction

  • Spending too much time on the internet.
  • Sneaking online when no one is watching.
  • Telling lies about how much time you spend online.
  • Not following the rules your parents set for the internet.
  • Staying up all night just to be online.

How to Stop Online Addiction

  • Use the computer only when you have a reason.
  • Agree with your family when you can use the internet and stick to those rules.
  • Parents can use special programs to help manage your time online.
  • Spend time doing fun things outdoors with your family.

For more information and help, you can visit Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline.
