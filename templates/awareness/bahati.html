Cyberbullying

Imagine online bullies are like villains from your favorite cartoon who tease, but don't let them spoil your day. Talk to a grown-up if you need help!

Online Grooming

Online grooming is when someone tries to trick you, just like a tricky character in a fairy tale. Always tell your parents or a guardian about any online friends you have!

Identity Theft

Identity thieves are like sneaky wizards pretend to be you. Keep your secrets safe, and don't share personal information with anyone!

Child-friendly content

Imagine your computer as a magic box, and you should never open any bad or scary pictures. Stay away from anything that doesn't look nice or friendly!

Protecting Yourself

Always remember to stay away from strangers online, just like you would in the real world. Only talk to people you know and trust, like your parents and friends!

For more information and help, visit Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline.
